About Us

EZ Foam & Insulation Colorado provides insulation services for homeowners, builders, and businesses throughout Pueblo and Southern Colorado. Our team works on homes, garages, shops, crawl spaces, metal buildings, additions, and commercial properties, helping customers improve comfort, reduce air leakage, and increase long-term energy efficiency in Colorado’s changing climate.


Whether you are insulating a new construction project, upgrading an older home, or improving temperature control in a detached shop or workspace, we focus on reliable service, clean installation, and insulation solutions designed for long-term performance. We proudly serve Pueblo, Pueblo West, Canon City, Florence, Walsenburg, Trinidad, and surrounding Southern Colorado communities.


We are dedicated to continuing this tradition of excellence, bringing unparalleled value to every project we undertake, whether it involves enhancing thermal resistance with spray foam, removing outdated insulation, or installing efficient batt insulation solutions. We are thrilled to offer our professional services to a variety of locations, and we want to ensure that you know exactly where we operate. Our team is dedicated to providing exceptional service to all of our clients, no matter where they are located.
